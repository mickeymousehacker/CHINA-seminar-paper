Capabilities are built three ways — each with a different durability profile

Mandatory transfer

JV / quid pro quo

Foreign tech for market access; an implicit "technology tax" leads firms to withhold their best capabilities (Holmes et al. 2015).

Organic accumulation

Vertical integration

Path-dependent tacit knowledge in routines that cannot be licensed or hired — structurally non-imitable (Teece et al. 1997).

Springboard M&A

Cross-border acquisition

Acquire developed-market assets to leapfrog latecomer disadvantages in technology and brands (Luo & Tung 2007).

Research gap: no prior work compares all three acquisition modes under a single technology discontinuity — this paper's contribution.
