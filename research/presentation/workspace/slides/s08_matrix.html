What to take away

  • BYD — slow but deep: three decades from cell chemistry to vehicle software; path-dependent, non-imitable (Teece et al. 1997).
  • Geely — fast and deep: acquisition compressed the timeline; CMA co-development, patent convergence (Konda et al. 2022).
  • SAIC — fast but shallow: 8.3% quality gain via JV transfer, yet rent cannibalization left no proprietary EV platforms (Bai et al. 2025; Howell 2018).

Source: Bai et al. (2025); Zheng et al. (2022); Konda et al. (2022); Teece, Pisano & Shuen (1997)
