Useful but bounded — and inexpensive at roughly €21 per month

Where it was most helpful

  • Handled research volume unmanageable by hand
  • Caught three genuine data errors pre-submission
  • Persistent base survived a hardware failure

Real limitations

  • No access to paywalled journals
  • Geely's PDFs not machine-readable → more web research
  • Token limits hit quickly on the stronger model
  • AI formulations needed active critical review

Cost: Claude Pro at ~€21 / month per member · no per-query charges · no paid third-party data services.
