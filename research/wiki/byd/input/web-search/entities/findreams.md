# FinDreams

**BYD's family of component-supply subsidiaries, formally created from December 2019, covering batteries, powertrain, electronics, lighting, and moulding — the operational backbone of [[byd-company]]'s [[vertical-integration]] strategy.**

## Overview

In early 2020, [[byd-company]] formally transitioned its internal parts and components operations into distinct subsidiary companies under the FinDreams brand. This structural move served two strategic purposes: (1) making internal transfer pricing transparent and auditable, and (2) enabling external sales to third-party automotive OEMs without creating direct brand conflict with the BYD vehicle brand. [[2026-05-22-byd-vertical-integration-web-research]]

The FinDreams brand umbrella covers:

**FinDreams Battery Co., Ltd.** — Registered 5 May 2019 as successor to BYD Lithium Battery Co. Ltd. (established 1998). Produces consumer batteries, electronic batteries, EV batteries, and energy storage batteries. As of 2024, FinDreams Battery was the world's second-largest EV battery producer with 17% market share, behind CATL. The [[blade-battery]] is its flagship product for EVs. BorgWarner signed an 8-year supply agreement with FinDreams Battery for Blade Battery cells, demonstrating third-party demand. [[2026-05-22-byd-vertical-integration-web-research]]

**FinDreams Powertrain Co., Ltd.** — Develops and produces engines, transmissions, axles, electric car platforms, and PHEV systems. Manufactures high-efficiency permanent magnet motors and complete e-drive systems, enabling tight integration of battery, power electronics, and mechanical systems. [[2026-05-22-byd-vertical-integration-web-research]]

**FinDreams Technology Co., Ltd.** — Develops and produces automotive electronics and chassis-related parts for passenger cars, commercial vehicles, and rail transit. [[2026-05-22-byd-vertical-integration-web-research]]

## Key facts

- Created: December 2019 (formal registration), operational restructuring completed early 2020 [[2026-05-22-byd-vertical-integration-web-research]]
- Battery market share (2024): 17% global EV battery, #2 behind CATL [[2026-05-22-byd-vertical-integration-web-research]]
- External customers: BorgWarner (8-year Blade Battery cell supply deal); other OEMs [[2026-05-22-byd-vertical-integration-web-research]]
- Strategic tension: external sales enable revenue diversification but signal conflict of interest to competing car OEMs considering FinDreams as a supplier [[2026-05-22-byd-vertical-integration-web-research]]
