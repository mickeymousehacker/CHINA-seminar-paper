# CATL

**The world's largest EV battery manufacturer; ranked #1 globally with the largest market share, against which [[findreams]] Battery (BYD's battery subsidiary) ranks #2 at 17%.**

## Overview

CATL (Contemporary Amperex Technology Co., Limited) is the world's leading EV battery producer by volume and market share. In 2024, it held the top position globally, with [[findreams]] Battery (BYD's battery subsidiary) ranking second at 17% market share. [[2026-05-22-byd-vertical-integration-web-research]]

CATL represents an alternative model to BYD's fully integrated approach: CATL is a pure-play battery supplier that sells to multiple automotive OEMs, whereas BYD's battery operations serve primarily its own vehicles (with limited external sales under the FinDreams brand). This distinction is strategically important: CATL has no conflict-of-interest barrier to selling to any automaker, giving it a larger addressable market. [[2026-05-22-byd-vertical-integration-web-research]]

The relationship between CATL and BYD is both competitive (both pursue battery technology leadership) and illustrative (they represent opposite ends of the integration spectrum in battery supply).
