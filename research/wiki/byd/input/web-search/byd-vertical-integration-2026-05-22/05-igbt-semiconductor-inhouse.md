## Result 3
- **Title**: BYD's Secret Weapon: The Vertical Integration Advantage Explained
- **URL**: https://evparts4x4.com/blogs/news/byd-the-vertical-integration-advantage-explained
- **Content**:

IGBTs are the second most expensive component in an EV after the battery, accounting for 7–10% of the total manufacturing cost. Through BYD Semiconductor, the company makes its own critical chips, including special transistors called IGBTs, which are high-power switches that control electricity flow from the battery to the motor.

BYD is known to procure up to 70% of components from its in-house subsidiaries. Vertical integration has allowed the company to shorten the development lifecycle and lower overall costs by spending more time on research and solving potential integration problems ahead of time.

BYD's in-house chip capability gave the company a huge advantage during the global chip shortage of 2021-2022, as while competitors like Ford and General Motors had to stop production lines, BYD kept making cars and even increased production.
