- **Content**:

In 2024, BYD's revenue reached 777.1 billion yuan, marking a 29% year-on-year increase. In USD terms the company made revenue of $107.87 billion in 2024, an increase over 2023's $84.92 billion.

Net profit attributable to shareholders hit 40.25 billion yuan in 2024, up 34% year on year.

BYD's gross margin reached a record-high 19.4% in fiscal year 2024, reflecting a modest increase from 18.6% in 2023. BYD's operating profit margin rose slightly to 6.5% in 2024, compared to 6.4% in the prior year.

Since fiscal year 2019, BYD's gross profit has surged nearly 500%, climbing from 21 billion yuan to 151 billion yuan in just five years.

As of the end of 2024, BYD's cash reserves reached a record 154.9 billion yuan, while interest-bearing debt decreased from 36.55 billion yuan in 2023 to 28.58 billion yuan, accounting for just 4.9% of total liabilities.

BYD's R&D expenditure reached 54.2 billion yuan in 2024, an increase of 36% year on year.
