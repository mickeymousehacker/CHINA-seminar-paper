## Result 2
- **Title**: What is BYD DiLink: The intelligent operating system for BYD cars
- **URL**: https://en.todoandroid.es/What-is-BYD-DiLink--the-intelligent-operating-system-for-BYD-cars/
- **Content**:

BYD DiLink is a proprietary, Android-based, fully customizable infotainment system. Its main engine is based on the Android source code, though it's BYD's own adaptation, which gives BYD full control over the system's features and evolutions, allowing for deep customization and full integration with vehicle functions.

Launched in 2018 and now deployed in millions of vehicles worldwide, DiLink has evolved from a smart entertainment portal into a deeply integrated digital platform that enhances driving safety, user experience, and ecosystem connectivity across BYD, Denza, and Yangwang product lines.

DiLink includes rotating screen, app store, voice control, and OTA remote updates. DiLink's philosophy is based on four key dimensions: hardware, software, ecosystem and mobile connectivity.

At its 2024 Dream Day event, BYD unveiled a new "Xuanji" Architecture integrating electrification with intelligence, the intelligent connected system "DiLink," and the smart driver assistance system "DiPilot."

BYD stated that the right direction for smart NEV development is not only smart cockpits and smart driving, but also smart vehicles integrating electrification with intelligence.

This demonstrates BYD's vertical integration strategy, combining hardware, software platforms, battery technology, and autonomous driving capabilities into a unified intelligent vehicle ecosystem.

BYD obtained a conditional testing license for L3 autonomous driving on high-speed roads in Shenzhen in July 2023, making it the first car company to receive such a license in China.
