## Result 1
- **Title**: Why BYD Has a Real Edge in the Global EV Race
- **URL**: https://www.fool.com/investing/2025/10/30/why-byd-has-a-real-edge-in-the-global-ev-race/
- **Content**:

BYD leads the world in EV volume and controlled roughly 22% of the global new-energy vehicle market in 2024.

BYD delivered 4.27 million vehicles in 2024 and captured a commanding 34.1% market share in China's EV market.

Scale drives down unit costs, letting BYD price aggressively while still protecting margins, and creates extreme learning-curve efficiencies -- every vehicle BYD builds makes the next one cheaper and faster to produce.

BYD is exporting its manufacturing model globally by setting up plants in Asia and Latin America, with each site strategically positioned to avoid tariffs, shorten supply routes, and meet local demand.
