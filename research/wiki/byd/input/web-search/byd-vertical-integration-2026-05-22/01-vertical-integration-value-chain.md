- **Content**:

BYD designs and manufactures batteries, semiconductors, and even its own logistics systems. More specifically, BYD produces its own batteries (notably the Blade Battery), power electronics, motors, and semiconductors in-house.

This in-house production allows BYD to control costs and speed up production, reducing reliance on third-party suppliers and ensuring timely delivery. Additionally, during recent global supply chain crises, BYD's self-sufficient production model allowed it to maintain production levels while competitors struggled with semiconductor shortages and logistics challenges.

In 2024, BYD overtook Tesla in global EV sales, signaling a significant shift in the industry.
