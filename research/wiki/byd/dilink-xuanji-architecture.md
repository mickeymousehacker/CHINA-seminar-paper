# DiLink & Xuanji Architecture

**BYD's proprietary software and intelligent vehicle stack: DiLink is the in-car Android-based operating system (launched 2018), DiPilot is the autonomous driving assistance system, and Xuanji is the 2024 unified architecture integrating electrification with intelligence across all three layers.**

## Summary

DiLink, launched in 2018, is [[byd-company]]'s proprietary, Android-based infotainment and vehicle OS. It is BYD's own adaptation of Android source code, giving BYD full control over features, update cycles, and deep vehicle function integration — capabilities that would be constrained if BYD relied on a third-party OS provider. DiLink includes a rotating touchscreen, app store, OTA remote update capability, and voice control. It is deployed across BYD, Denza, and Yangwang vehicle lines and has evolved through multiple generations since 2018. [[2026-05-22-byd-vertical-integration-web-research]]

At BYD's 2024 Dream Day event, the company unveiled the "Xuanji" architecture — a unified framework integrating electrification with intelligence across three systems: DiLink (intelligent cockpit), DiPilot (smart driver assistance/autonomous driving), and the vehicle's electrification platform. BYD stated: "the right direction for smart NEV development is not only smart cockpits and smart driving, but also smart vehicles integrating electrification with intelligence." [[2026-05-22-byd-vertical-integration-web-research]]

BYD's approach to intelligent driving is consistent with its broader [[vertical-integration]] philosophy: rather than integrating a third-party system (as many Chinese OEMs use Huawei's HarmonyOS Cockpit or NVIDIA DRIVE), BYD builds the intelligence stack in-house. This also positions BYD competitively against pure-software automotive entrants like Huawei-backed AITO and Xiaomi. In July 2023, BYD became the first car company to receive a conditional L3 autonomous driving testing license on high-speed roads in China (Shenzhen). [[2026-05-22-byd-vertical-integration-web-research]]
