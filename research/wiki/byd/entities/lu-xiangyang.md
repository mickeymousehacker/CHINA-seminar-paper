# Lu Xiangyang

**Co-founder and Vice Chairman of [[byd-company]]; the second-largest founding-team shareholder through his firm Youngy Investment Holding Group Co., Ltd.**

## Overview

Lu Xiangyang is the co-founder of BYD and serves as Vice Chairman on the board. While [[wang-chuanfu]] is the more prominent public face and operational leader, Lu holds a significant ownership stake that reinforces founder control over the company. Lu's investment firm, Youngy Investment Holding Group Co., Ltd., holds approximately 5.11% of BYD Company Limited, in addition to his direct personal stake. Together with Wang Chuanfu's ~17.6%, the two founding partners control well over one-third of total voting rights, making external challenges to strategic direction effectively impossible without their alignment.
