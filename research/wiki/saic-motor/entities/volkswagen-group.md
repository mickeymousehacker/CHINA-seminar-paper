# Volkswagen Group

**German automotive OEM; 50/50 JV partner of SAIC Motor in SAIC-VW since 1984.**

## Key facts

- JV with SAIC: [[saic-volkswagen]] (50/50, since 1984, extended to 2040)
- Also holds VW–FAW JV (since 1991) — produces Audi and Jetta brands
- SAIC-VW produces Passat and Tiguan; no product-line overlap with VW–FAW [[2026-06-03-bai-barwick-cao-li-2025-quid-pro-quo-aer]]
- SAIC-VW capacity utilization 2025: 55% (1.058m of 1.920m designed) [[2026-06-03-saic-annual-report-2025]]
- MEB electric platform plant utilization 2025: 14% (34,464 of 240,000 designed) [[2026-06-03-saic-annual-report-2025]]
