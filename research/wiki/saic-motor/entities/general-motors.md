# General Motors

**US automotive OEM; 50/50 JV partner of SAIC Motor in SAIC-GM since 1997.**

## Key facts

- JV with SAIC: [[saic-gm]] (50/50, since 1997)
- Produces Buick and Chevrolet brands in China via SAIC-GM
- SAIC-GM sales collapsed −56.54% in 2024 to 435,007 units [[2026-05-23-saic-annual-report-2024]]
- GM's delegation to China in 1978 introduced the JV concept to Chinese policymakers [[2026-06-03-bai-barwick-cao-li-2025-quid-pro-quo-aer]]
