# BYD Company

**Private Chinese EV-native automaker; primary competitive benchmark for SAIC Motor in the EV transition.**

## Key facts

- Privately owned (no JV affiliation in Bai et al. dataset — classified as non-affiliated domestic)
- EU anti-subsidy tariff: 17.0% (vs. SAIC 35.3%) — reflects lower assessed subsidy intensity [[2026-06-03-eu-regulation-2024-2754-bev-tariffs]]
- ROE 2024: ~22% (vs. SAIC 3.43% in 2025, 0.58% in 2024)
- Proprietary battery platform (Blade Battery) and DM-i hybrid system — capabilities SAIC lacks
