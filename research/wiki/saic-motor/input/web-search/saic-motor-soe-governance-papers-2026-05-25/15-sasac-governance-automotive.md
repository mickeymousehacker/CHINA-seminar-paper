- **URL**: https://asiasociety.org/sites/default/files/inline-files/ASNC_Rhodium_SOEReport.pdf
- **Content**:

Asia Society / Rhodium Group policy report PDF. "MISSING LINK: Corporate Governance in China's State Sector." Non-academic policy report. Not an ABS-ranked peer-reviewed journal article.

Notes from search summary: SASAC was created in 2003 by the Hu Jintao administration to improve group-level SOE corporate governance. A parallel network of SASACs operates at the provincial level and in major municipalities, including Shanghai. In Shanghai, the structure involves coordination between city government officials and Shanghai SASAC leadership.
