## Result 2
- **Title**: Corporate ownership, efficiency and performance under state capitalism: Evidence from China - ScienceDirect
- **URL**: https://www.sciencedirect.com/science/article/abs/pii/S0161893818300486
- **Content**:

ScienceDirect article (Journal of Corporate Finance, 2018). Examines corporate ownership, efficiency, and performance under state capitalism in China. Finds state-ownership negatively impacts firm performance attributable to agency costs, ineffective strategic management, and administrative task fulfillment. Notes SOEs in general underperform private enterprises, but SOEs controlled by central government outperformed all types of firms.
