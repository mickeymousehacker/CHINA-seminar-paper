## Result 1
- **Title**: Dynamic study of corporate governance structure and firm performance in China | Chinese Management Studies | Emerald Publishing
- **URL**: https://www.emerald.com/cms/article/13/2/299/29258/Dynamic-study-of-corporate-governance-structure
- **Content**:

Emerald article in Chinese Management Studies. Study of corporate governance structure and firm performance in China. Finds no relationship between board size (including independent directors) and firm performance, but CEO duality has a significantly negative effect on firm performance, while concentration of ownership has a significantly positive influence.

## Result 3
- **Title**: The Impacts of Corporate Governance Mechanisms and Ownership Structure on Firm Performance: A Case Study of Chinese Dual-Listed Companies | Advances in Decision Sciences
- **URL**: https://journal.iads.site/index.php/ADS/article/view/192
- **Content**:

Advances in Decision Sciences article. Examines corporate governance mechanisms and ownership structure on firm performance in Chinese dual-listed companies. Finds state and legal entity shares are significantly negatively related to Tobin's Q, while foreign shares are positively related to it.
