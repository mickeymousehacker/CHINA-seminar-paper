- **Title**: (PDF) State-owned Enterprises Around the WORLD as Hybrid Organizations
- **URL**: https://www.researchgate.net/publication/276121669_State-owned_Enterprises_Around_the_WORLD_as_Hybrid_Organizations
- **Content**:

ResearchGate PDF record. Publication 276121669. Full citation: Bruton, G. D., Peng, M. W., Ahlstrom, D., Stan, C., & Xu, K. (2015). "State-owned enterprises around the world as hybrid organizations." Academy of Management Perspectives, 29(1), 92-114.

Key content as reported: State-owned enterprises represent approximately 10% of global gross domestic product but remain relatively underexplored by management scholars. The paper argues for viewing firms not dichotomously as either state owned or privately owned, but rather as hybrid organizations in which the levels of ownership and control by the state can vary. The research draws on 36 cases from four industries in 23 countries to provide a richer understanding of state-owned enterprises.
