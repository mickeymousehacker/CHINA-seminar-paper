IDEAS/RePEC record. Publisher: Oxford University Press. Journal: The Quarterly Journal of Economics. Volume 109, year 1994, issue 4, pages 995-1025. Authors: Andrei Shleifer, Robert W. Vishny.

Key content as reported: The paper presents a model of bargaining between politicians and managers that explains stylized facts about state firms, their commercialization, and privatization. The model shows how subsidies to public enterprises and bribes from managers to politicians emerge naturally, and uses it to understand why commercialization and privatization might work and what forces contribute to effective restructuring of public enterprises.
