# SAIC Motor Financial Data 2020–2024 (Web Research)

**Comprehensive financial data for SAIC Motor Corporation Limited (SSE: 600104) covering revenue, net profit, operating profit, vehicle sales volumes, NEV sales by brand, and JV equity income indicators for fiscal years 2020–2024, gathered from official investor relations pages, annual report PDFs, and financial data aggregators.**

## Key takeaways

- Revenue declined from a CNY 779.8 B peak (2021) to CNY 627.6 B in 2024 (−15.73% YoY), with the steepest single-year drop in 2024.
- Net profit attributable to shareholders collapsed from CNY 24.5 B (2021) to CNY 1.67 B (2024) — a ~93% five-year decline — driven by SAIC-GM impairment provisions (23.212 B yuan) and the China EV price war.
- Total vehicle sales fell from 5.02 million (2023) to 4.01 million (2024); NEV sales grew counter-cyclically to 1.234 million (+9.9% YoY wholesale).
- IM Motors 2024: 66,000 units (+71.2% YoY); 2023: 38,300 units (+665% YoY from tiny base).
- SAIC-GM equity investment balance declined CNY 6.338 B (−9.5%) from end-2023 to end-2024, reflecting JV losses absorbed via equity method.
- Annual Report 2024 PDF confirmed available at saicmotor.com (English version).

## Data flags and uncertainties

- **EPS conflict (2024)**: StockAnalysis reports EPS 0.14; Annual Report 2024 search snippet reports 0.71. Likely due to difference between A-share count and total share count, or H-share inclusion. Verify against PDF.
- **Net profit unit ambiguity (2024)**: Search result reported "16.66 billion RMB" which is inconsistent with StockAnalysis CNY 1,666 million = 1.666 B. The likely interpretation is 1.666 B CNY (StockAnalysis), with the "16.66" figure using 100M yuan units (a common Chinese financial reporting convention). Treat StockAnalysis CNY 1,666 M as the best available figure pending PDF verification.
- **Operating income discrepancy**: StockAnalysis (GAAP-adjusted) vs. Yahoo Finance figures differ substantially for 2023 and 2024. May reflect JV equity income treatment.
- **Wikipedia brand table**: Shows "IM" at 903,789 units for 2023, inconsistent with official SAIC press release figure of 38,300 IM Motors units. Wikipedia appears to mislabel a brand category. Official SAIC press release is authoritative.
- **Statista JV data**: Behind paywall; snippets only for SAIC-GM revenue (2022: 162.8 B yuan) and sales (2023: ~1M units).
- **Annual report PDFs not directly parsed**: PDFs available at saicmotor.com (see [[urls]] in source folder) but not fetched. All figures above from aggregated search results and intermediary financial sites.
