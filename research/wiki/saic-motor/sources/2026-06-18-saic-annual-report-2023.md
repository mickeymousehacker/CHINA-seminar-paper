# SAIC Motor Annual Report 2023

**SAIC Motor's 2023 Annual Report documents stable revenues but declining profitability, with SAIC-GM already under pressure at 53% capacity utilization — the baseline against which the 2024 collapse must be read.**

**Full citation**: SAIC Motor Corporation Limited. (2024). *Annual Report 2023*. Shanghai Stock Exchange (Stock Code 600104). PricewaterhouseCoopers Zhong Tian LLP.

---

## Key financial data (2023)

- **Revenue**: RMB 726.20 billion (+0.72% YoY)
- **Total revenue incl. finance**: RMB 744.71 billion (+0.09% YoY)
- **Net profit attributable to shareholders**: RMB 14.11 billion (−12.48% vs. 2022)
- **Net profit ex non-recurring items**: RMB 10.04 billion (+11.71% vs. 2022)
- **Weighted average ROE**: 4.98% (2022: 5.84%)
- **Basic EPS**: RMB 1.226 (2022: 1.400)
- **Auditor**: PricewaterhouseCoopers Zhong Tian LLP (unqualified opinion)

## Key operational data (2023)

### Vehicle sales / capacity utilization
| Plant | Designed capacity (10k units) | Production (10k units) | Utilization |
|---|---|---|---|
| SAIC Volkswagen | 208.8 | 120.2 | **58%** |
| SAIC General Motors | 190.8 | 101.8 | **53%** |
| SAIC Passenger Vehicle | 104.0 | 98.9 | 95% |
| SAIC GM Wuling | 176.0 | 140.4 | 80% |

- **SAIC-GM production 2023**: ~1,018,000 units (101.8 × 10,000) — baseline for 2024 comparison
- **SAIC-GM 2024**: 435,007 units (−56.54%) — decline from ~1,018,000 to 435,007

### NEV data (2023)
- Total NEV sales: 1,122,908 units (+4.61% YoY)
- VW MEB Plant utilization: 44% (106,547 of 240,000 designed)

## Relevance for SAIC Motor paper

| Dimension | How to use |
|---|---|
| **Financing** | SAIC-GM already at only 53% utilization in 2023 — the 2024 collapse (−56.54% to 435,007 units) was not sudden but accelerated a pre-existing trend. Cite for baseline comparison. |
| **Long-term Viability** | VW MEB plant at 44% utilization in 2023, falling to 14% by 2025 — structural EV demand failure visible even in 2023. |
| **Capability Building** | NEV sales 1.12m in 2023, growing to 1.37m in 2024 and 1.64m in 2025 — own-brand EV trajectory positive but from small base. |
