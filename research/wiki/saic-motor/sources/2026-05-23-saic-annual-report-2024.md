# SAIC Motor Annual Report 2024

**Official annual report of SAIC Motor Corporation Limited (SSE: 600104), audited by PricewaterhouseCoopers Zhong Tian LLP. Covers fiscal year 2024 financials, governance, shareholder structure, JV operations, strategy, and R&D.**

## Key takeaways

- Net profit collapsed 88.19% to RMB 1.666 billion, driven mainly by a massive SAIC-GM asset impairment provision in Q4 2024.
- Total vehicle sales fell 20.07% to 4.013 million units; SAIC-GM alone dropped 56.54%.
- Shanghai SASAC is the ultimate controller via SAIC Group (63.27% stake) — a classic state-led governance model.
- SAIC-VW joint venture extended 10 years to 2040 despite declining sales.
- NEV retail sales hit 1.368 million (+29.9% YoY) — a bright spot, but not yet profitable enough to offset JV losses.
- Total R&D (incl. JVs): RMB 35.16 billion (+7.32% YoY); cumulative EV/intelligence investment >RMB 150 billion.

## Claims

### Governance & Ownership
- Actual controller: Shanghai State-owned Assets Supervision and Administration Commission (Shanghai SASAC), directly under Municipal Government of Shanghai. [[2026-05-23-saic-annual-report-2024]]
- Controlling shareholder: Shanghai Automotive Industry (Group) Co., Ltd. holds **63.27%** (7,324,009,279 of 11,575,299,445 shares). [[2026-05-23-saic-annual-report-2024]]
- Other major shareholders: China COSCO Shipping 5.87%, Yuejin Motor Group 3.58%, China Securities Finance Corp 3.02%, Shanghai International Group 2.48%. [[2026-05-23-saic-annual-report-2024]]
- Listed on Shanghai Stock Exchange as A shares (code: 600104). Total shares: 11,575,299,445. [[2026-05-23-saic-annual-report-2024]]
- Board dominated by state appointees; legal representative: Wang Xiao Qiu (Chairman). [[2026-05-23-saic-annual-report-2024]]

### Financial Performance 2024
- Total revenue: RMB 627.59 billion (2023: RMB 744.71 billion, **−15.73%**). [[2026-05-23-saic-annual-report-2024]]
- Auto manufacturing revenue: RMB 614.07 billion (−15.44%); gross margin 9.38% (−0.11 pct). [[2026-05-23-saic-annual-report-2024]]
- Net profit attributable to shareholders: RMB 1.666 billion (2023: RMB 14.106 billion, **−88.19%**). [[2026-05-23-saic-annual-report-2024]]
- Net profit excl. non-recurring: RMB **−5.409 billion** (2023: +10.045 billion, −153.85%). [[2026-05-23-saic-annual-report-2024]]
- Basic EPS: RMB 0.145 (2023: 1.226; 2022: 1.400). [[2026-05-23-saic-annual-report-2024]]
- Weighted average ROE: **0.58%** (2023: 4.98%; 2022: 5.84%). [[2026-05-23-saic-annual-report-2024]]
- Total assets: RMB 957.14 billion. Net assets (shareholders): RMB 287.84 billion. [[2026-05-23-saic-annual-report-2024]]
- Operating cash flow: RMB 69.27 billion (+63.62% YoY — driven by inventory reduction and SFC loan adjustments). [[2026-05-23-saic-annual-report-2024]]
- R&D expenses (standalone): RMB 17.65 billion (3.55% of revenue). Capitalized: RMB 4.16 billion. [[2026-05-23-saic-annual-report-2024]]

### Quarterly breakdown (2024)
- Q1: RMB +2.71 billion net profit
- Q2: RMB +3.91 billion net profit
- Q3: RMB +0.28 billion net profit
- Q4: RMB **−5.24 billion** net profit (SGM write-down dominates) [[2026-05-23-saic-annual-report-2024]]

### Reasons for profit collapse
1. Vehicle sales volume declined YoY; price war compressed gross margins.
2. SAIC-GM (SGM) made large asset impairment provision in Q4 2024 → investment income fell 52%.
3. MG Motor India (renamed JSW MG Motor India) equity transfer boosted non-recurring items but underlying operations worsened. [[2026-05-23-saic-annual-report-2024]]

### Strategy & JVs
- SAIC-VW JV extended to **2040** on 40th anniversary; plans 18 new models by 2030; new AUDI brand launched. [[2026-05-23-saic-annual-report-2024]]
- Self-owned brand retail: 2.741 million vehicles (~60% of total, +5 pct vs. 2023). [[2026-05-23-saic-annual-report-2024]]
- NEV retail: 1.368 million (+29.9% YoY) — new historical high. [[2026-05-23-saic-annual-report-2024]]
- Overseas retail: 1.082 million (+2.6% YoY); European sales 240,000+ (record despite EU tariff). [[2026-05-23-saic-annual-report-2024]]
- EU anti-subsidy tariff on SAIC: **36.3%** (highest among Chinese OEMs). [[2026-05-23-saic-annual-report-2024]]
- Roewe + Rising brands integrated; MG brand focused on key models (ES5, MG7). [[2026-05-23-saic-annual-report-2024]]
- Robotaxi test licenses obtained for urban roads and elevated expressways. [[2026-05-23-saic-annual-report-2024]]
- First to mass-produce first-generation solid battery; 26,000 valid patents held. [[2026-05-23-saic-annual-report-2024]]
- Cumulative investment in electrification/intelligence: >RMB 150 billion. [[2026-05-23-saic-annual-report-2024]]
- Total R&D incl. JVs: RMB 35.16 billion (+7.32%); R&D staff: 30,600 (17.5% of total employees). [[2026-05-23-saic-annual-report-2024]]
