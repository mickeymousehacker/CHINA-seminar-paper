## Concepts

- [[capital-market-financing]] — Funding growth via repeated equity issuances; NIO's model; causes sustained shareholder dilution.
- [[cma-platform]] — Compact Module Architecture; shared Geely-Volvo platform; flagship output of post-acquisition co-development.
- [[cross-border-ma]] — Acquisition of developed-market firms by emerging-market acquirers to obtain strategic assets; Geely's core model.
- [[emerging-market-acquirer]] — Latecomer firm from an emerging economy using M&A to rapidly close capability gaps.
- [[jv-governance]] — Equity joint ventures between Chinese and foreign automakers for market access and technology sharing; SAIC's model.
- [[post-acquisition-integration]] — Four-stage model (distancing → balancing → building → diversifying) documented in Geely-Volvo case.
- [[strategic-asset-seeking-fdi]] — FDI motive focused on acquiring technology, brands, and know-how rather than markets or labour.
- [[technology-transfer]] — Knowledge and capability absorption by acquirer from acquired firm; primary mechanism of Geely-Volvo value creation.
- [[vertical-integration]] — Manufacturing >70% of components in-house; BYD's model; delivers cost and quality control.
