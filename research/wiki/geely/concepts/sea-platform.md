# SEA Platform

**Sustainable Experience Architecture — Geely's purpose-built battery electric vehicle (BEV) platform, underpinning ZEEKR, Smart brand vehicles (via Smart Automobile JV), and Geometry models.**

## Summary

The Sustainable Experience Architecture (SEA) platform is Geely's dedicated BEV architecture, designed to complement the earlier [[cma-platform]] (which was co-developed with Volvo Cars for electrified ICE/PHEV vehicles). SEA was developed internally by Geely's engineering team as a skateboard-style EV platform capable of supporting a wide wheelbase and body style range [[2026-06-19-geely-annual-report-2023]].

SEA underpins multiple Geely ecosystem brands. ZEEKR vehicles — including the ZEEKR 001, 009, and X — are all built on SEA. The Smart brand vehicles developed in the Geely/Mercedes-Benz 50:50 JV also use SEA as their engineering base, developed by Geely's global engineering network [[2026-06-19-geely-annual-report-2023]]. The platform also supports the Geely Geometry sub-brand (consolidated March 2023) and some Galaxy models.

SEA represents Geely's transition from acquired platform technology (licensing or adapting Volvo-origin architectures) to proprietary platform development — marking a maturation of the post-Volvo technology transfer strategy into indigenous innovation capability.
