# Smart Geely 2025 Strategy

**Geely Automobile Holdings' multi-year corporate strategy launched in 2021, targeting 3.65 million vehicle sales annually by 2025 and structured around seven technology pillars for intelligent and electrified vehicle development.**

## Summary

The "Smart Geely 2025" strategy was released by Geely Automobile Holdings in 2021 to guide the company's transition from a volume-driven ICE automaker to a technology-led intelligent mobility group. The strategy sets a sales volume target of 3.65 million vehicles per annum — roughly double the FY2023 group level — and establishes seven core technology domains as the capability investments necessary to achieve it [[2026-06-19-geely-annual-report-2023]].

The seven pillars are: (1) intelligent driving, (2) intelligent cockpits, (3) electronic/SEA architecture, (4) battery systems, (5) powertrain (hybrid and pure EV), (6) smart manufacturing technology, and (7) active and passive safety. The strategy reflects Geely's response to the accelerating "intelligence-ification" (智能化) and electrification trend in the Chinese auto market, where software-defined vehicles and advanced driver assistance systems (ADAS) have become key competitive differentiators [[2026-06-19-geely-annual-report-2023]].

In execution, Smart Geely 2025 underpins the Galaxy NEV brand launch (February 2023) and the Geometry consolidation (March 2023) — both product-portfolio moves designed to address the mass-market electrification opportunity without cannibalising the Geely brand. The strategy also guides ZEEKR's premium positioning and the group's investment in Geely Global Comprehensive Safety Centre [[2026-06-19-geely-annual-report-2023]].
