# Technology Transfer and the Internationalization Process of the Emerging Market Firm (2020)

**Book chapter examining the mechanisms of knowledge and technology transfer in the Geely-Volvo post-acquisition integration, using the 2010 acquisition as the empirical case.**

## Key takeaways
- Documents concrete technology transfer mechanisms: integrated greenfield plants, joint engine plant, supply-system co-ordination, management/engineering integration, joint R&D, and the CMA platform.
- The chapter's contribution is the analysis of transfer mechanisms, not the four-stage integration model (which is Zheng et al. 2022).

## Claims
- CMA (Compact Module Architecture) is the flagship output of the Geely-Volvo co-development process.
- Technology transfer was enabled by the high degree of autonomy granted to Volvo Cars post-acquisition.
