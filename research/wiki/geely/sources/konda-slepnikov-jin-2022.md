# From Transaction to Co-Creation in Geely's Acquisition of Volvo Cars (2022)

**Journal article demonstrating via longitudinal patent portfolio and market performance analysis that Geely-Volvo achieved genuine post-acquisition co-creation — a rare outcome among emerging-market acquirers.**

## Key takeaways
- Only a few EMNEs achieve genuine co-creation with an acquired partner; Geely-Volvo is a confirmed rare success case.
- Patent portfolio convergence between Geely and Volvo over time is measurable evidence of knowledge integration.
- Co-creation requires mobilizing and enhancing knowledge resources on both sides, not merely transferring them one-way.
- Published online 14 October 2022.

## Claims
- Geely-Volvo integration progressed "from transaction to co-creation" — the title phrase characterising the wiki's integration concept.
- Patent convergence analysis confirms successful knowledge integration quantitatively.
- Market performance analysis corroborates co-creation outcomes.
