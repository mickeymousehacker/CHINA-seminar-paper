# The Effects of Outbound M&As on Chinese Automobile Corporations' Performance (2021)

**Journal article evaluating whether Geely's acquisition of Volvo improved acquirer performance over the long run, establishing a post-acquisition performance evaluation framework for Chinese automotive M&A.**

## Key takeaways
- Geely's post-acquisition financial and operational performance improved measurably over both short and long run.
- The paper proposes a performance evaluation framework applicable to other Chinese automotive outbound M&As.
- Supports outbound M&A as a viable growth mechanism for Chinese automakers.

## Claims
- Post-acquisition performance assessment covers both immediate and prolonged outcomes.
- Geely-Volvo is used as an instructive case for other Chinese automotive M&A assessments.
