# Geely Automobile Holdings — 2025 Annual Report (FY2024)

**The official annual report of Geely Automobile Holdings Limited (HK: 0175) for the fiscal year ended 31 December 2024, disclosing record revenue of approximately RMB 240bn, record net profit of RMB 16.8bn, total group vehicle sales of 2.17 million units, and strategic updates across all portfolio brands.**

## Key takeaways

- Geely Automobile Holdings posted a record FY2024 net profit of RMB 16.8bn (+240% YoY) and basic EPS of RMB 1.64 (+219%), the strongest annual result in the company's listed history.
- Group vehicle sales reached approximately 2.17 million units in 2024, with the Geely brand core, Lynk & Co, ZEEKR, and international markets (Proton, LEVC) all contributing growth.
- ZEEKR was consolidated into Geely Auto's balance sheet following its NYSE IPO in May 2024; the Lynk & Co ownership structure was simultaneously rationalised to ZEEKR 51% / Geely Auto 49%.
- Polestar remained in severe financial distress (going-concern qualification, net loss USD 1.19bn FY2023), with ZGH acting as guarantor for USD 800m in bank facilities; the annual report period captures continued restructuring.
- Lotus Technology (Nasdaq: LOT) suffered a 44% revenue decline in FY2025 (its own fiscal year) and a 46% fall in deliveries to 6,520 units; Geely exercised a put option requiring Lotus Technology to absorb 100% of Lotus UK, pending close in 2026.
- The FY2024 dividend payout ratio was 42.03%, with a proposed 50% increase in the final dividend per share — signalling management confidence in earnings sustainability.
- Strategic priorities stated in the report emphasise NEV (new energy vehicle) acceleration, international expansion via Proton and LEVC, and further integration of the premium EV portfolio under the ZEEKR umbrella.
