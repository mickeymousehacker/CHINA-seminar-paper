# Geely Automobile Holdings — FY2025 Annual Report

**The official annual report of Geely Automobile Holdings Limited (HK: 0175) for the fiscal year ended 31 December 2025, reporting revenue of approximately CN¥ 345.2bn, core profit attributable to owners of the parent of RMB 14.41bn (+36% YoY), total group vehicle production of approximately 3,024,567 units (exceeding the 2,710,000-unit target), and strategic completion of ZEEKR's privatisation from NYSE.**

## Key takeaways

- Geely Automobile Holdings delivered FY2025 core profit attributable to owners of the parent of RMB 14.41bn, up 36% year-on-year — the second consecutive year of strong profit growth following FY2024's record result.
- Group revenue reached approximately CN¥ 345.2bn in FY2025 (~USD 47.6bn at 7.25 CNY/USD), implying approximately 44% YoY growth relative to FY2024's confirmed CN¥ 240.2bn — driven by the first full consolidated year of ZEEKR revenues and strong core Geely brand demand.
- Total group production reached approximately 3,024,567 vehicles in FY2025, significantly exceeding the 2,710,000-unit sales target set at the start of the year; the 2026 sales target is set at 3,450,000 units.
- ZEEKR's privatisation from NYSE was completed in December 2025, returning it to full ZGH/Geely Auto ownership; this consolidation simplifies the group structure and eliminates minority shareholder obligations from the premium EV segment.
- Lynk & Co delivered approximately 350,495 vehicles in FY2025 (+22.8% YoY), consolidating its role as the group's mid-premium growth brand; in March 2026, Volvo Cars was designated the exclusive European importer for Lynk & Co vehicles.
- The proposed FY2025 final dividend implies a dividend per share of approximately CN¥ 0.33 (+50% YoY from FY2024's CN¥ 0.22), consistent with management's signalled commitment to higher shareholder returns.
- US reporting obligations under the Securities Exchange Act were terminated in December 2025, reflecting the ZEEKR delisting and the group's strategic shift away from US capital markets.

**Revenue note:** The FY2025 revenue of CN¥ ~345bn vs. FY2024's CN¥ 240.2bn implies ~44% YoY growth. A large portion of this is structural: ZEEKR was only consolidated into Geely Auto from May 2024, meaning FY2024 included ~8 months of ZEEKR revenue while FY2025 includes the full 12 months. ZEEKR's FY2023 standalone revenue was USD 7.28bn (~CN¥ 52bn); on a full-year basis for FY2025, ZEEKR's revenue contribution (at flat volumes) would be similar in scale, partially explaining the revenue step-up from CN¥ 240bn to CN¥ 345bn. The organic sales volume growth (from ~2.17m units in FY2024 to ~3.02m in FY2025, +39%) also contributed.

**Profit note:** "Core profit attributable to owners" (RMB 14.41bn, +36%) is the figure confirmed from the official press release. This is a non-GAAP metric that excludes certain items (e.g., fair value changes on investments, one-off gains from ZEEKR-related transactions in FY2024). Eulerpool estimates total net income at CN¥ 16.49bn for FY2025, implying non-core items added approximately CN¥ 2bn above the core profit figure. The FY2024 annual report reported profit attributable to equity holders of RMB 16.63bn, which was boosted by IPO-related gains from ZEEKR's NYSE listing in May 2024; with no comparable one-off in FY2025, the reduction in total profit to ~CN¥ 16.5bn (if Eulerpool is correct) while core profit rose 36% is internally consistent.

**ZEEKR volume note:** ZEEKR brand deliveries were essentially flat at 224,133 (FY2025) vs. 222,123 (FY2024). The Wikipedia article on Zeekr lists "574,628 vehicles (2025)" as ZEEKR Intelligent Technology Group's total production, which includes Lynk & Co (350,495) vehicles since ZEEKR now holds 51% of Lynk & Co. The 224,133 figure is ZEEKR-brand vehicles only.
