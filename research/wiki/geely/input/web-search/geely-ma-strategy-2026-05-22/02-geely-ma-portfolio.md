## Synthesized search engine summary (verbatim from search results)

**Major Acquisitions**

Geely acquired 100% of Volvo Car Corporation from Ford Motor Company on August 2, 2010, for $1.8 billion.

Geely acquired London Taxi (now called London Electric Vehicle Company; LEVC) in 2013, and in May 2017, ZGH purchased a 51% controlling-stake in Lotus Cars from its owner, DRB-HICOM, with the remaining 49% acquired by Etika Automotive.

**Joint Ventures**

Geely Holding owns or holds stakes in Geely Auto, Volvo Cars, Polestar, Lotus, ZEEKR, Lynk & Co, London Electric Vehicle Company (LEVC), PROTON, Smart (joint venture with Mercedes-Benz), and Farizon Auto for commercial vehicles.

In August 2017, ZGH, Geely Automobile Holdings and Volvo Car Group signed an agreement to establish the Lynk & Co joint venture. In 2018 Geely established a JV with Contemporary Amperex Technology (CATL), to research and develop battery cells.

**Overall Strategy**

Between 2012 and 2021, Geely made several acquisitions and embarked upon a number of joint ventures, including taking a stake in Malaysia's Proton Holdings and the UK's Lotus, as well as Germany's Daimler and France's Renault.
