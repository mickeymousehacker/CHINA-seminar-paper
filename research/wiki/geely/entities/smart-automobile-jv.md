# Smart Automobile Co., Ltd.

**Global 50:50 joint venture between Geely Holding and Mercedes-Benz (Daimler) for the Smart brand, established January 2020, with RMB 5.4bn ($777m) registered capital and headquarters in Hangzhou Bay, Ningbo, China.**

## Overview

Smart Automobile Co., Ltd. was formally established in January 2020 after receiving regulatory approval. Each party contributed RMB 2.7bn to a registered capital of RMB 5.4bn ($777m). The JV is structured as a true 50:50 governance arrangement with equal board representation [[2026-05-22-geely-ma-strategy-web-research]].

The board consists of six directors, three nominated by each party. Geely board seats are held by: Li Shufu (Chairman, ZGH), An Conghui (President ZGH / CEO Geely Auto), and Daniel Donghui Li (CFO ZGH). Daimler seats are held by: Hubertus Troska (Greater China board member), Britta Seeger (Marketing & Sales), and Markus Schäfer (R&D) [[2026-05-22-geely-ma-strategy-web-research]].

The CEO is Tong Xiangbei, overseeing all operations: sales, marketing, R&D, production, and after-sales. The product strategy combines Mercedes-Benz Design (global design network) with Geely's engineering network for development. Manufacturing is based in China [[2026-05-22-geely-ma-strategy-web-research]].

The JV represents Geely's shift from pure acquisition to co-equal partnership with a Western OEM — contrasting with the full-control model of the Volvo acquisition.
