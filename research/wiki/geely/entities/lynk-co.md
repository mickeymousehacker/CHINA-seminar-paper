# Lynk & Co

**Chinese-European automotive brand and subscription model, established as a joint venture in August 2017 among ZGH, Geely Automobile Holdings, and Volvo Car Group; vehicles built on the jointly developed CMA platform.**

## Overview

Lynk & Co was established by a JV agreement signed in August 2017 between ZGH, Geely Automobile Holdings, and Volvo Car Group. The brand deploys the [[cma-platform]] co-developed by Geely and Volvo and is positioned between mainstream Geely and premium Volvo in the brand hierarchy. Lynk & Co is noted for its subscription-based sales model [[2026-05-22-geely-ma-strategy-web-research]].
