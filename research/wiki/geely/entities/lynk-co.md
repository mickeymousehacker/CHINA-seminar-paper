# Lynk & Co

**Chinese-European automotive brand and subscription model, established as a joint venture in August 2017 among ZGH, Geely Automobile Holdings, and Volvo Car Group; vehicles built on the jointly developed CMA platform; equity restructured November 2024 with ZEEKR acquiring majority control.**

## Overview

Lynk & Co was established by a JV agreement signed in August 2017 between ZGH, Geely Automobile Holdings, and Volvo Car Group. The brand deploys the [[cma-platform]] co-developed by Geely and Volvo and is positioned between mainstream Geely and premium Volvo in the brand hierarchy [[2026-05-22-geely-ma-strategy-web-research]].

The original equity structure was: Geely Auto 50%, Volvo Car Group 30%, ZGH 20%. In November 2024, this was restructured: [[zeekr]] acquired Volvo's 30% stake plus 21% from Geely Auto, giving ZEEKR 51% of Lynk & Co while Geely Auto retained 49%. This consolidation placed Lynk & Co under the ZEEKR premium-EV group umbrella, rationalising the Geely group's mid-to-premium EV portfolio [[2026-06-19-geely-annual-report-2023]] [[2026-06-19-geely-annual-report-2025]].

In FY2022, Lynk & Co delivered 180,127 vehicles, maintaining its role as the group's mid-premium volume brand despite the broader headwinds from COVID supply chain disruptions affecting the Chinese auto market [[2026-06-19-geely-annual-report-2022]]. Lynk & Co has shown consistent sales growth in China: 180,127 units in FY2022, 220,250 in FY2023 (+22%), and 285,441 in FY2024 [[2026-06-19-geely-annual-report-2023]]. In Europe, however, the subscription model has faced losses — approximately 1 billion Swedish kronor (about EUR 100m) accumulated between 2020 and 2021 — though Chinese volumes have offset group-level concerns [[2026-06-19-geely-annual-report-2023]].

The brand offers plug-in hybrids and BEVs in Europe exclusively, operating subscription centres across Sweden, Netherlands, Belgium, Germany, Spain, France, and Italy, alongside 378 retail centres in China and dealer networks across 25 countries/regions [[2026-06-19-geely-annual-report-2023]].
