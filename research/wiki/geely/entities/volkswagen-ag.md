# Volkswagen AG

**German automotive conglomerate; 40% equity partner in SAIC-Volkswagen (founded 1984), one of the longest-standing Sino-foreign automotive JVs in China, extended to 2040.**

## Key facts
- SAIC-VW equity: VW AG 40%, VW China Invest 10%, SAIC 50% [[2026-05-22-geely-ma-strategy-web-research]]
- JV partnership with SAIC extended to 2040 [[2026-05-22-geely-ma-strategy-web-research]]
- SAIC-VW 2021 revenues: ~RMB 159bn (-8.7% YoY) [[2026-05-22-geely-ma-strategy-web-research]]
