# Li Shufu

**Founder, Chairman, and sole private owner of Zhejiang Geely Holding Group; the architect of Geely's M&A-driven global expansion strategy.**

## Overview

Li Shufu (also known in English as Eric Li) is the founder and chairman of [[zhejiang-geely-holding-group]] (ZGH). He is the ultimate controlling party of both ZGH (private, 100% ownership) and, through ZGH, of the publicly listed [[geely-automobile-holdings]] (HK: 0175) [[2026-05-22-geely-ma-strategy-web-research]].

Li Shufu secured a controlling stake in Guorun Holdings in January 2004, which was subsequently renamed Geely Automobile Holdings Limited in March 2004 — the vehicle for the Hong Kong listing. His governance model employs family trusts and holding vehicles for estate and control planning, while preserving board nomination rights at the listed entity [[2026-05-22-geely-ma-strategy-web-research]].

He personally served on the six-member board of directors of the Smart Automobile Co., Ltd. 50:50 JV alongside Daimler, representing Geely alongside An Conghui (Geely Holding President) and Daniel Donghui Li (Geely CFO) [[2026-05-22-geely-ma-strategy-web-research]].

Li Shufu is widely credited with executing the contrarian 2010 bid for Volvo Cars — then regarded as a distressed asset — and with the subsequent "run Volvo as Volvo" autonomous management philosophy that academic research cites as key to preserving Volvo's innovation capacity [[2026-05-22-geely-ma-strategy-web-research]].
