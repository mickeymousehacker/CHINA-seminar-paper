# Polestar

**Swedish-Chinese premium EV brand, jointly controlled by Zhejiang Geely Holding Group and Volvo Cars; listed on Nasdaq US (PSNY).**

## Overview

Polestar emerged from Volvo Cars' performance division and was spun out as a standalone EV brand under joint ZGH/Volvo Cars ownership. It is listed on Nasdaq US under the ticker PSNY.
