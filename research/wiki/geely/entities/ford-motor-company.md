# Ford Motor Company

**American automaker; sold Volvo Car Corporation to Zhejiang Geely Holding Group in 2010 for USD 1.8bn, providing USD 200m of seller financing as part of the transaction.**

## Overview

Ford Motor Company acquired Volvo Cars in 1999 for ~$6.45bn as part of its "Premier Automotive Group" strategy. After significant losses during the 2008–2009 financial crisis, Ford divested Volvo to [[zhejiang-geely-holding-group]] in 2010 for USD 1.8bn — a dramatic write-down that created the opportunity for Geely's strategic acquisition [[2026-05-22-geely-ma-strategy-web-research]].

Ford structured USD 200m of the $1.8bn purchase price as a seller note, with the remainder paid in cash. The sale was announced March 28, 2010 and completed August 2, 2010 [[2026-05-22-geely-ma-strategy-web-research]].
