# Mercedes-Benz

**German premium automotive and commercial vehicle group; co-established the 50:50 Smart Automobile Co., Ltd. joint venture with Zhejiang Geely Holding Group in January 2020 to revive the Smart brand as a global all-electric marque.**

## Overview

Formerly Daimler AG (renamed Mercedes-Benz Group AG in 2021), Mercedes-Benz entered a 50:50 joint venture with [[zhejiang-geely-holding-group]] to rescue the Smart brand by converting it to an all-electric, China-built, globally sold vehicle. The JV was established with RMB 5.4bn ($777m) registered capital in January 2020 [[2026-05-22-geely-ma-strategy-web-research]].

Mercedes-Benz's board representatives on the Smart JV are Hubertus Troska (Greater China), Britta Seeger (Marketing & Sales), and Markus Schäfer (R&D). Design of new Smart models is performed by the Mercedes-Benz global design network; engineering by Geely [[2026-05-22-geely-ma-strategy-web-research]].
