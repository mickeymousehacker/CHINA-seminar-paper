# LEVC

**London Electric Vehicle Company, formerly London Taxi Company; acquired by Zhejiang Geely Holding Group in 2013; manufacturer of iconic black cabs now converted to range-extended electric powertrains.**

## Overview

Geely acquired LEVC (then known as London Taxi Company, manufacturer of the iconic London black cab) in 2013. Under Geely ownership, LEVC transitioned from traditional TX4 combustion cabs to the TX electric range-extender vehicle, with a manufacturing facility in Coventry, UK [[2026-05-22-geely-ma-strategy-web-research]].

LEVC is a wholly owned ZGH subsidiary, representing a smaller-scale acquisition compared to Volvo and Lotus but following the same pattern: acquiring a heritage Western brand and transforming it with Chinese capital and EV technology.
