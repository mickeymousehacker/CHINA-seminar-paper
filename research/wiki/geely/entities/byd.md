# BYD

**Chinese automotive and battery conglomerate that has become the world's largest NEV producer through a deep vertical integration strategy, manufacturing over 70% of vehicle components in-house including its proprietary Blade Battery.**

## Overview

BYD Company Limited is the paradigm case of the vertically integrated Chinese automaker, contrasted against Geely's M&A-driven model and SAIC's JV model. BYD's strategy centres on owning the entire value chain: batteries (Blade Battery), power electronics, motors, and semiconductors are all produced in-house, providing unmatched control over quality, cost, and supply chain stability [[2026-05-22-geely-ma-strategy-web-research]].

This model has delivered exceptional financial results. In FY2024, BYD reported revenue of RMB 777bn ($107bn, +29% YoY) and net income of RMB 40.25bn ($5.55bn, +34% YoY). Gross margins held near 20% despite aggressive pricing competition. BYD delivered 4.27 million vehicles in 2024, surpassing Tesla in plug-in vehicle deliveries [[2026-05-22-geely-ma-strategy-web-research]].

Academic research (ResearchGate/AJMSS: "The Motivation and Economic Consequences of BYD's Vertical Integration Strategy") analyses the strategic rationale and financial consequences of this approach [[2026-05-22-geely-ma-strategy-web-research]].
