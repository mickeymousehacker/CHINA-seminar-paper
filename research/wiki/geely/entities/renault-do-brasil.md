# Renault do Brasil

**Brazilian subsidiary of Renault Group managing vehicle manufacturing operations in Brazil; Geely Automobile Holdings acquired a 26.4% equity stake in FY2024 as part of ZGH's emerging-market international expansion strategy.**

## Overview

Renault do Brasil is the Brazilian arm of Renault Group, operating manufacturing facilities in the Paraná state of Brazil (São José dos Pinhais). Geely Automobile Holdings Limited acquired a 26.4% stake in Renault do Brasil during FY2024, representing the first material Geely investment in South American automotive manufacturing [[2026-06-19-geely-annual-report-2024]].

This acquisition extends ZGH's cross-border M&A and partnership strategy beyond its historical focus on European (Volvo, Lotus, LEVC, Smart) and Southeast Asian (Proton, Malaysia) markets into Latin America.

## Key facts

- Geely Automobile Holdings acquired 26.4% equity stake in FY2024 [[2026-06-19-geely-annual-report-2024]]
