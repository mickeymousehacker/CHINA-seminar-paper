# ZEEKR

**Premium Chinese EV brand launched by Zhejiang Geely Holding Group; NYSE listed May 2024 (ticker: ZK); privatised and delisted December 2025 when Geely Auto acquired all public float; now wholly owned by Geely Automobile Holdings, holds 51% of Lynk & Co, and fully integrated Lynk & Co into the ZEEKR group in February 2025.**

## Overview

ZEEKR is ZGH's premium all-electric brand targeting the upper segment of the Chinese EV market. It was positioned to compete with NIO, BMW, and Tesla. Its vehicles — including the ZEEKR 001, 007, 009, X, 7X, 8X, Mix, and 9X models — are built on Geely's proprietary [[sea-platform]] (Sustainable Experience Architecture) [[2026-06-19-geely-annual-report-2023]].

ZEEKR launched commercially in April 2021 with the ZEEKR 001 as its debut model. FY2022 was its first full calendar year of deliveries, during which it delivered 71,941 vehicles — a dramatic increase from the approximately 6,007 units delivered in the partial launch year of 2021 [[2026-06-19-geely-annual-report-2022]].

As of 31 December 2023, Geely Automobile Holdings held 62.8% of ZEEKR. ZEEKR completed its NYSE IPO in May 2024 (ticker: ZK), raising approximately USD 441m — described as the largest Chinese company NYSE IPO since 2021 — at an implied valuation consistent with the February 2023 funding round valuation of USD 13bn [[2026-06-19-geely-annual-report-2023]].

By December 2025, Geely Auto completed the acquisition of all remaining public shares in a privatisation transaction valued at approximately USD 2.4bn in total, and ZEEKR's delisting from NYSE was finalised [[2026-06-19-geely-annual-report-2025]] [[2026-06-19-geely-h1-2025-interim-results]]. US reporting obligations were simultaneously terminated.

In February 2025, Lynk & Co was fully integrated into the ZEEKR group (announced July 2025 interim results context). The combined ZEEKR Intelligent Technology Group (ZEEKR brand + Lynk & Co) produced approximately 574,628 vehicles in FY2025 (224,133 ZEEKR brand + 350,495 Lynk & Co brand) [[2026-06-19-geely-annual-report-2025]].

ZEEKR's delivery volumes grew strongly year-on-year: 71,941 (FY2022) → 118,685 (FY2023, +65%) → 222,123 (FY2024, +87%) → 224,133 (FY2025, essentially flat as the brand prioritised margin over volume post-privatisation) [[2026-06-19-geely-annual-report-2022]] [[2026-06-19-geely-annual-report-2023]] [[2026-06-19-geely-annual-report-2025]].
