# Lotus Cars

**British sports car and technology brand; ZGH acquired a 51% controlling stake in May 2017 from DRB-HICOM; now operates as Lotus Technology Inc. and is listed on Nasdaq US (ticker: LOT); in severe financial distress as of FY2025 with a 44% revenue decline and Geely/Etika put-option exercises triggering a full Lotus UK consolidation.**

## Overview

Zhejiang Geely Holding Group (ZGH) purchased a 51% controlling stake in Lotus Cars from Malaysian conglomerate DRB-HICOM in May 2017. The remaining 49% was acquired by Etika Automotive (another Malaysian entity). This gave Geely operational control while preserving a local partnership structure [[2026-05-22-geely-ma-strategy-web-research]].

Lotus has been repositioned from a pure sports car brand toward a broader performance EV and technology company. It is now branded as Lotus Technology Inc. and is listed on Nasdaq US. The Lotus Technology Form 6-K is available via SEC EDGAR (CIK 0001962746) [[2026-05-22-geely-ma-strategy-web-research]].

This acquisition illustrates ZGH's playbook of acquiring heritage Western automotive brands with strong intellectual property and design cachet, then leveraging Chinese manufacturing scale and capital to fund transformation. However, the Lotus case has proved significantly more difficult than the Volvo acquisition: revenue fell 44% in FY2025 (Lotus Technology's own fiscal year) to USD 519m, deliveries collapsed 46% to 6,520 units, and the accumulated deficit reached approximately USD 3.2bn [[2026-05-22-geely-ma-strategy-web-research]].

In April 2025, Geely exercised a put option requiring Lotus Technology to acquire Geely's 51% stake in Lotus UK (the British operations entity). In June 2025, Etika exercised its own put option on the remaining 49% of Lotus UK. The result is that Lotus Technology is now obligated to acquire 100% of Lotus UK, with closing expected in 2026. A strategic pivot was also announced: Lotus Technology launched its first PHEV (the "For Me") in China in March 2025 — a retreat from its original EV-only positioning [[2026-05-22-geely-ma-strategy-web-research]] [[2026-06-19-geely-annual-report-2025]].
