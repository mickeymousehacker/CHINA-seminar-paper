# Lotus Cars

**British sports car and technology brand; ZGH acquired a 51% controlling stake in May 2017 from DRB-HICOM; now operates as Lotus Technology and listed on Nasdaq US.**

## Overview

Zhejiang Geely Holding Group (ZGH) purchased a 51% controlling stake in Lotus Cars from Malaysian conglomerate DRB-HICOM in May 2017. The remaining 49% was acquired by Etika Automotive (another Malaysian entity). This gave Geely operational control while preserving a local partnership structure [[2026-05-22-geely-ma-strategy-web-research]].

Lotus has since been repositioned from a pure sports car brand toward a broader performance EV and technology company. It is now branded as Lotus Technology Inc. and was listed on Nasdaq US (SEC-registered). The Lotus Technology Form 6-K (FY2025) is available via SEC EDGAR [[2026-05-22-geely-ma-strategy-web-research]].

This acquisition illustrates ZGH's playbook of acquiring heritage Western automotive brands with strong intellectual property and design cachet, then leveraging Chinese manufacturing scale and capital to fund transformation.
