# Geely Automobile Holdings

**The publicly listed automotive arm of Zhejiang Geely Holding Group, traded on the Hong Kong Stock Exchange (HK: 0175) and in the US OTC market (GELYY/GELYF).**

## Overview

Geely Automobile Holdings Limited (HK: 0175) is the listed vehicle through which Li Shufu's private holding group, [[zhejiang-geely-holding-group]] (ZGH), accesses public capital markets. It was formed in March 2004 when Li Shufu renamed Guorun Holdings (in which he had secured a controlling stake in January 2004) to facilitate a Hong Kong listing [[2026-05-22-geely-ma-strategy-web-research]].

Governance follows a single-class share structure (one share, one vote) with no dual-class shares. ZGH holds ~39.94% directly, and Proper Glory Holding Inc. holds a further ~26.2%; together these ZGH-related vehicles account for at least 76% of Geely Auto's shares. Major institutional investors include BlackRock (4.57%) and Vanguard (2.49%) [[2026-05-22-geely-ma-strategy-web-research]].

The listed entity is distinct from the broader Geely Holding portfolio. Volvo Cars, for instance, is owned at the Zhejiang Geely Holding level, not by the listed Geely Auto, though the listed arm contributed equity financing for the 2010 Volvo acquisition.

Financially, Geely Auto reported revenue of $33.4bn in 2024 (+32% YoY), with profit surging 240% to RMB 16.8bn and basic EPS climbing 219% to RMB 1.64. The 2024 dividend payout ratio was 42.03%, with a proposed 50% increase in final dividend per share [[2026-05-22-geely-ma-strategy-web-research]].
