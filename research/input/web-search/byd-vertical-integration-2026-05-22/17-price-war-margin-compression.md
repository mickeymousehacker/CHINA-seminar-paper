BYD's net profit margin rose from 1.66% in 2019 to 5.20% in 2023 but now faces reversal. The company's Q2 2025 net profit plummeted 29.9% year-on-year to 6.4 billion yuan, marking its first quarterly decline in over three years, despite a 14% revenue increase to 200.9 billion yuan.

BYD's gross margin declined from 21.4% in 2023 to 17.3% in Q1 2024. BYD's gross margins further compressed to 10–15%, far below Tesla's 18% and Li Auto's 20.1% in Q2 2025.
