- **Content**:

## Result 3
- **Title**: BYD Has An Aggressive Plan To Expand Into Foreign Markets
- **URL**: https://cleantechnica.com/2025/11/10/byd-has-an-aggressive-plan-to-expand-into-foreign-markets/
- **Content**:

A key factor in BYD's expansion is an effort to mitigate against the risk of tariffs and other trade barriers by assembling their cars in factories abroad. BYD localized production in areas such as Thailand, Uzbekistan, Turkey, and Hungary in addition to Brazil.

Turkey has emerged as a crucial hub for European market access, with BYD investing approximately $1 billion in a facility capable of producing 150,000 vehicles a year, as Turkey's customs union with the EU means vehicles produced there can enter European markets tariff-free.

Exports are expected to account for about 20% of total sales in 2025, up from 10% a year earlier.

BYD sold more electric cars than Tesla in Europe for the first time, despite tariffs imposed by the European Union.
