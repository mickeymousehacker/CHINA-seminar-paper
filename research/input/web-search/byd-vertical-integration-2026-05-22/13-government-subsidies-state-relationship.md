## Result 3
- **Title**: BYD and the Subsidy Trap: How China's EV Giant Exploits Government Support
- **URL**: https://www.rafaelbenavente.com/p/byd-and-the-subsidy-trap-how-chinas
- **Content**:

There is a strong positive correlation between government subsidies and BYD's EV sales, with increased subsidies significantly driving sales growth. However, state subsidies explain just 5% of BYD's cost advantage over Tesla, with scale, lower-cost talent, and vertical integration doing the rest.

The connection between BYD and Chinese government is deep and multifaceted. The CCP has called on BYD to "go out" and conquer foreign markets, and it has supported the company's efforts through military collaboration, funding, and heavy subsidies. The company operates closely with the Ministry of Industry and Information Technology (MIIT) and has seen former officials appointed to board or advisory roles.
