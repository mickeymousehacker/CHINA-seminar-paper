## Result 1
- **Title**: BYD Company - Wikipedia
- **URL**: https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/BYD_Company
- **Content**:

In early 2020, BYD transitioned its parts and components division into subsidiary companies, collectively known as FinDreams.

The establishment of FinDreams companies from December 2019 focused on power batteries, automotive lighting, electronics, powertrain, and moulding.

FinDreams comprises the following divisions:

**Battery Division:** FinDreams Battery Co., Ltd. was registered on 5 May 2019 as a successor to BYD Lithium Battery Co. Ltd., which was established in 1998. Its products include consumer batteries, electronic batteries, electric vehicle batteries and energy storage batteries. Through its subsidiary, FinDreams Battery, BYD was the world's second-largest electric vehicle battery producer in 2024, holding a 17% market share, behind CATL.

**Powertrain Division:** FinDreams Powertrain Co., Ltd. develops and produces engines and powertrain-related parts such as transmissions, axles, electric car platforms and plug-in hybrid systems.

**Electronics Division:** FinDreams Technology Co., Ltd. develops and produces automotive electronics and chassis-related parts that are used in passenger cars, commercial vehicles, and rail transit.

This vertical integration structure allows BYD to control key components of electric vehicle production internally while also serving as a supplier to other automotive manufacturers globally.
